About

The Woman’s Club of Redondo Beach has been adored and celebrated for nearly a century. Truly a special place to create treasured memories, The Woman’s Club Event Center offers so much more than an average venue for events. Visit us and vision your next creative commemoration.

The Woman's Club of Redondo Beach offers a beautiful atmosphere for special events of all types, including weddings and receptions, quinceañeras, banquets, anniversary soirees, holiday parties and more. In addition to private parties, the venue is an excellent location for corporate and business meetings, training seminars, classes and workshops.

The main ballroom can accommodate up to 200 guests and features a large traditional dance floor, perfect for celebrating your special day! And what about live music? Showcase your favorite band, feature a concert or performance on our grand stage. Adjacent to our open ballroom is a lovely garden area, ideal for intimate wedding ceremonies, or simply enjoying fresh air and mingling with guests.
