    Timeline

    Our Story

    • 1988-1996

      Our Humble Beginnings

      DJ Ricardo V started DJing at the age of 13 in the South Bay. Back then it was strictly vinyl Records. It wasn’t easy finding records in my neighborhood.

    • 1996-2008

      WP Records

      At the age of 21, I established my own personal DJ shop in the city of Lomita. This marked the birth of WP Records, a hub catering to all DJ needs, which operated until 2008.

    • 2008

      Event Planning

      As time changed, so did the business. It eventually transitioned into an events company. Knowing and working with so many vendors in our industry allowed us to serve our clients better as we could offer them many services under one roof.

    • 2023

      Cre8tive Wedding and Events

      Fast forward to 2023, we've been in the industry for over 25 years, specializing in DJs, MCs, and Wedding Planning. Now known as Cre8tive Weddings and Events, or Cre8tiveLA.com, your go-to for all event-related needs.

    · Venue Info ·

    Space 400 of Redondo Beach is the perfect venue for your Wedding Ceremony, Quinceañera, Birthday Party, Graduation Party, Rehearsal Dinner, or just about any other event!

    We can host up to 200 seated guests without a dance floor, or 130 seated guests with a dance floor .

    Available accommodations consist of the Main Ball Room with Restroom Access, alongside optional rental spaces such as a Kitchen and a Newly Renovated Outdoor Patio with Bistro Lighting, perfect for your wedding ceremony!

    The duration of each rental is for 6 hours, as well as a 2-hour window before the rental time for event decoration and an additional 1-hour grace period for post-event clean-up.

    Rental package includes Cleanup Services and a dedicated Staff Member for the day, responsible for setting up tables and chairs according to your preferences. At the conclusion of the event, the staff member will put tables and chairs into storage after clients have removed all items from the venue.

    Rental Options

    You can either rent the venue and bring your own food, drinks, and decorations, or you can opt for Cre8tiveLA to handle your event by providing rentals and managing all the planning, which is our expertise!
