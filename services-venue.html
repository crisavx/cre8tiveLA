Our Services

The Woman’s of Redondo Beach is the perfect place for your next memorable event. Specializing in tailored customer service, we go out of our way to ensure your occasion is a success!

Please Contact Us with any questions regarding our special setting. Our venue management offers some of these exclusive services:

EVENT MANAGEMENT - Individual on-site assistance with planning and logistics can be booked with our Event Manager.

EVENT HOURS - The WCRB Event Center is open and available to our clients exclusively on the day of your event Fridays and Saturdays from 9am to midnight and Sundays from 1pm to midnight. We have other days available please contact us for more info.

Contact us today to explore WCRB can provide just the right amount of warmth and charm to make your event memorable.

HABLAMOS ESPAÑOL - Our Event Manager can handle all of your questions and assist with special events tailored for our Latin community. Please ask about reserving your next Wedding, Party or Quinceañera.
