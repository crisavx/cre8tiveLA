We can host up to 200 seated guests without a dance floor, or 130 seated guests with a dance floor .

Available accommodations consist of the Main Ball Room with Restroom Access, alongside optional rental spaces such as a Kitchen and a Newly Renovated Outdoor Patio with Bistro Lighting, perfect for your wedding ceremony!

The duration of each rental is for 6 hours, as well as a 2-hour window before the rental time for event decoration and an additional 1-hour grace period for post-event clean-up.

Rental package includes Cleanup Services and a dedicated Staff Member for the day, responsible for setting up tables and chairs according to your preferences. At the conclusion of the event, the staff member will put tables and chairs into storage after clients have removed all items from the venue.
